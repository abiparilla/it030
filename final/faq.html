Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my skin type?

Try our Skin Care Type Quiz and find out! It's quick and helps to choose products and routines tailored to meet the specific needs of your skin.

How often should I update my skincare routine?

Update your routine seasonally with the changing seasons or because your skin requires it, having been influenced by age, stress, or other changes in your lifestyle.

Are the products suggested suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! All of the products we recommend are sensitive-skin and gentle-skin friendly, though we always advise patch testing beforehand.

Can I mix different skincare brands in my routine?

Yes! Just be sure the ingredients get along with one another. Our product page indicates any significant combinations to steer away from.
